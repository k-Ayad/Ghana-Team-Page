Our Mission in Ghana

The Coptic Orthodox Church has been blessed to establish a strong presence in Ghana, serving communities across the nation with the ancient faith and traditions that have been preserved for over 2000 years.

With 9 churches strategically located throughout Ghana, we are committed to spreading the Gospel, serving our communities, and building a vibrant spiritual family in West Africa.
